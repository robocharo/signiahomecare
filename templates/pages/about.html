Our Story

About Signia Home Care

A trusted Minnesota home care provider built on compassion, respect, and community.

Locally owned and operated in Minneapolis—serving the Twin Cities with personalized care since day one.

Compassionate Trusted Community-Centered

Signia Home Care was founded on a simple belief: everyone deserves to live with dignity in the place they call home. Based in Minneapolis, Minnesota, we serve individuals and families across the Twin Cities with personalized, compassionate care.

Who We Are

We are a locally owned and operated home care company committed to helping people stay safe, healthy, and independent at home. Our team includes skilled nurses, therapists, and trained caregivers who bring warmth and professionalism to every visit.

We serve adults, seniors, and individuals with disabilities throughout Minneapolis, Maple Grove, Minnetonka, Wayzata, and surrounding communities. Whether you need a few hours of support each week or more comprehensive care, we're here to help.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide dignified, person-centered home care that supports independence, safety, and quality of life for individuals and families across Minnesota.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted home care provider in the Twin Cities—known for compassion, reliability, and the difference we make in people's lives.
