Who Can Make a Referral

We welcome referrals from a wide range of healthcare and community partners. Our intake team is ready to assist you.

Hospitals & Discharge Planners

Facilitate smooth transitions from hospital to home. We coordinate with your discharge team to ensure continuity of care.

Social Workers & Case Managers

We work closely with care coordinators to match clients with the right services and support their care plans.

Physicians & Healthcare Providers

Physician referrals are prioritized. We'll keep you informed throughout the intake and care coordination process.

Families & Caregivers

Self-referrals welcome. If you or a loved one needs care at home, we're here to help you get started.

Community Organizations

Senior centers, churches, and community groups—we partner with organizations that serve our shared communities.

{# Process Steps #}

How the Referral Process Works

From submission to care start, we keep the process straightforward and transparent.

1

Submit the Form

Complete the referral form with client information and care needs. Required fields are marked. You can also call us directly for urgent referrals.

2

We Follow Up

Our intake team reviews referrals within 1–2 business days. We'll contact you to confirm receipt and discuss next steps.

3

Assessment & Planning

We assess the client's needs, verify insurance or funding, and create a personalized care plan. We'll keep you updated throughout.

4

Care Begins

When appropriate, we coordinate the start of care and introduce the care team. We remain in contact for ongoing coordination.

{# FAQ #}

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will you respond?

We review referrals within 1–2 business days and will contact you to confirm receipt. For urgent referrals, call us directly at {{ business.phone }}.

What information do I need to provide?

At minimum, we need your name and contact information. Client name, service interest, and care needs help us respond faster. You can provide as much or as little as you're comfortable sharing.

Can I refer myself or a family member?

Yes. Self-referrals and family referrals are welcome. We'll guide you through the intake process and answer any questions.

Do you accept Medicare and Medicaid?

We work with various funding sources including Medicare, Medicaid, private pay, and long-term care insurance. Our intake team can discuss options during the assessment.

What areas do you serve?

We serve Minneapolis and the greater Twin Cities area, including Maple Grove, Minnetonka, Wayzata, Edina, St. Louis Park, and surrounding communities.
